Welcome to The Urban Biodiversity Alliance

We are a passionate community organization dedicated to environmental action and biodiversity conservation, serving the Malden, Medford, Melrose, and surrounding areas. We focus on promoting sustainable agriculture, permaculture, urban farming, and sustainable technology while celebrating environmental victories and amplifying community efforts.

Through our events, discussions, and calls to action, we strive to keep things positive by posting victories for communities working toward environmental justice and indigenous rights. We connect with other biodiversity and environmental groups, hosting over 20 events and screening new films that inspire action.

Our goal is to create meaningful conversation threads and boost other groups' efforts, while building a network of local activists who want to stay informed and engaged in creating positive environmental change in our communities.
